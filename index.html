Money-Saving Tips
📅

Book vendors on weekdays or in off-peak seasons (Jan–Mar) for savings of 15–30% on venues and photographers.

🍽️

Choose a venue that includes in-house catering — package deals often save 20–30% over bringing in an external caterer.

🥂

Limit your bar to beer and wine instead of a full open bar — this alone can save $2,000–$5,000 at larger weddings.

🌸

Use seasonal and locally-grown flowers. They're fresher, cost far less, and your florist can suggest beautiful in-season alternatives.

📦

Ask vendors about package deals. Many photographers offer video add-ons at a discount, and florists may bundle décor and centerpieces.

💳

Put all wedding expenses on a rewards credit card (paid off immediately) to accumulate points for honeymoon flights or hotels.

📝

Set a firm "miscellaneous" buffer of 5–10% of total. Unexpected costs always arise — having this prevents budget panic.

📁

Keep all vendor contracts, receipts, and payment confirmations in one folder from day one. You'll thank yourself later.

Getting Started

How To Use This Planner

Everything you need to know to get the most out of every tab in your wedding planner. Start here if you're new, or come back anytime as a reference.

Quick Start — Do This First
01
Header
Enter Your Details
At the very top of the page, type your names and wedding date. This populates the countdown timer and personalises your exported data file.
💡 Pick a colour theme using the five swatches — your choice is saved automatically.
02
Budget Tracker
Set Your Total Budget
Go to the Budget tab and enter your total wedding budget in the large input box. Then adjust the per-category budget amounts to match your priorities. All totals calculate live.
💡 You can add, rename, recolour, and remove budget categories to match your exact wedding.
03
Vendors
Add Your Vendors
Head to Vendors to fill in contact details for each supplier. Set appointment dates and times, track deposit payments, and click the contract badge to cycle through Unsigned → Pending → Signed.
💡 Use the Compare tab to evaluate two or more options before booking.
04
Expenses
Log Every Payment
Every time you make a payment, add it in the Expenses tab. Choose the matching category and the Budget Tracker will update automatically — no manual entry needed there.
💡 Keep receipts and reference numbers here so you have a full paper trail.
All Tabs Explained
✦ Dashboard
Your Live Overview
See your total budget, amount spent, remaining funds, and a breakdown by category at a glance. All numbers update as you use other tabs.
💰 Budget
Allocate & Track
Set your overall budget, then assign amounts per category. The Spent column auto-fills from Expense Log entries. Add custom budget lines for anything not in the default list.
📋 Expenses
Record Every Payment
Log payments with date, vendor, category, amount, payment method, and receipt number. Entries automatically update category spending in Budget and Dashboard.
📇 Vendors
Contact Directory
One card per vendor — business name, contact person, phone, email, deposit, appointment date & time, and contract status. Click the status badge to cycle between Unsigned, Pending, and Signed.
⚖️ Compare
Side-by-Side Decisions
Name a category (e.g. "Venue"), choose how many slots, then fill in each option's name, price, pros/cons, and availability. Mark a winner with the ⭐ button. Comparisons are saved.
📅 Payments
Never Miss a Deadline
Schedule upcoming deposits and balance payments with due dates. Click any status pill to cycle it through Upcoming → Pending → Paid → Overdue. Rows are sorted by due date automatically.
💐 Guests
Guest List & Catering
Four calculators work together: enter your estimated adult and child counts for an early budget projection, then add guests to the list and mark RSVPs — the Confirmed Guests card and catering totals update automatically as people confirm. Both an estimated and a confirmed catering total are shown side by side so you can track how your actual headcount compares to your original plan.
💡 ~85% of invited guests typically confirm — use this as your starting estimate.
🪑 Seating
Visual Table Plan
Click Add Table, name it, and set a capacity. Then drag guests from the Unassigned sidebar onto tables. Drag the tables themselves to reposition them on the canvas.
💡 Guests must be added in the Guests tab first to appear in the unassigned list.
💍 Wedding Party
Bridal Party Details
Track all members — bridesmaids, groomsmen, flower girls, and more. Store their role, contact info, attire details, and measurements in one place.
⏱️ Day Schedule
Minute-by-Minute Timeline
Build your complete wedding day itinerary with times, events, locations, and who's involved. Items are sorted by time automatically and displayed on a clean visual timeline. Delete any item with the × button. Print or share the full schedule with your vendor team.
💡 Add every vendor handover time — photographer arrival, caterer setup, florist delivery — so everyone knows exactly when to be where.
🎁 Gifts
Gift & Thank-You Tracker
Log each gift with who it's from, a description, value, and date received. Click the Thank You badge to toggle between Pending and Sent — so no one is missed.
✈️ Honeymoon
Post-Wedding Getaway
Set a honeymoon budget, split it across flights, hotel, activities, food, and extras. Enter your destination and travel dates to see the number of nights automatically calculated.
🎵 Music
Playlist Planner
Assign songs to specific moments — processional, first dance, reception entrance, last dance, and more. Share the completed list directly with your band or DJ.
📸 Shot List
Photography Shot List
Pre-loaded with must-have photo categories. Each shot is fully editable — click any item to rename it to suit your day. Add custom shots with the + Add field at the bottom of each category, and remove any you don't need with the × button.
💡 Share this list with your photographer before the day so nothing gets missed.
🧳 Packing
Three Packing Lists
Separate lists for the Wedding Day Bag, Rehearsal Dinner, and Honeymoon. Tick items off as you pack. Pre-loaded with common essentials — nothing forgotten.
✅ Checklist
Month-by-Month Tasks
A timeline-based planning checklist covering 12+ months through to 1 month before. Click any task to tick it off, and use the Add Task field to add your own custom items to any phase.
Saving, Exporting & Importing
💾 Auto-Save
Always Saved Locally
All your data is saved automatically to your browser's local storage every time you make a change. The "All changes saved ✓" toast confirms each save. Note: clearing your browser data will erase this — use Export to back up.
⬇️ Export Data
Download a Backup
Click Export Data in the footer to download a .json file containing all your data. Do this regularly — especially before clearing browser history. The filename includes your names and today's date.
⬆️ Import Data
Restore from Backup
Click Import Data in the footer and select a previously exported .json file. Your data will be restored immediately. This also lets you move your planner between devices or browsers.
🗑️ Clear All Data
Full Reset
The Clear All Data button in the footer permanently resets the planner. You must type DELETE to confirm. Always export a backup first if you want to keep your data.
Frequently Asked Questions
Will my data be saved if I close the browser?▼
Yes — data is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. As long as you don't clear your browser data or use a different browser/device, everything will be there when you return. For peace of mind, use Export Data to create a backup file.
Can I use this on multiple devices?▼
Yes! Export your data on one device (using the footer button), then open the planner on your other device and use Import Data to load the file. Repeat whenever you want to sync between devices.
How do I add a vendor that isn't in the default list?▼
In the Vendors tab, click the pink "+ Add New Vendor" button. Type whatever category/role you like — florist, travel agent, event stylist, anything. The new card will appear in the grid alongside the defaults.
How does the Expense Log connect to the Budget Tracker?▼
When you add an expense and assign it to a category (e.g. "Photography & Video"), the Spent column for that category in Budget Tracker updates automatically. You don't need to enter amounts in both places.
Can I rename or delete the default budget categories?▼
You can delete any category by clicking the small × button on the right side of each row in the Budget Tracker. To add a custom category, use the "+ Add Custom Budget Line" button at the bottom. Renaming isn't supported yet — delete and re-add with your preferred name.
How does the Seating Plan work?▼
First add your guests in the Guests tab. Then go to the Seating Plan tab, click "+ Add Table" to create tables, and drag guest chips from the Unassigned sidebar onto any table. You can also drag tables around the canvas to arrange your floor plan. Click the × next to a seated guest to unassign them.
Is this planner private? Does anyone else see my data?▼
Completely private. All data lives only in your browser's local storage on your own device. Nothing is sent to any server. Erana Banana has no access to any information you enter here.
